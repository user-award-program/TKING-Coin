Our Project

The dApps

ChainChat

ChainChat aims to be a private peer-to-peer messenger over the blockchain centered around each user’s wallet address. Users will have the ability to send encrypted messages to specified wallet addresses, which can then be retrieved by proving ownership of the address via Web3 validation methods.

Beta Live Now

EtherLift

EtherLIFT is a gas fee sponsorship application that bridges the gap between advertisers and cryptocurrency. After completing a transaction, users can watch ads in return for credits that can be used for ETH, NFT’s, or discount codes for products from affiliated brands. $TKING will be partnering with EtherLIFT to expand its outreach.

Watch Demo

Tiger Tracks

TigerTracks Blockchain is a graphical representation of the data objects contained within the blockchain. Featuring a navigable exploding cloud-map diagram, the user will gain the ability to visually track and explore transactions, holdings, and interactions on the blockchain. The transactional connections between wallets, Exchanges, contracts and other objects are visually weighted to represent the number and size of transactions, in much the same way that the data found in a heat map diagram can be quickly and effectively digested by all.

Tiger King Coin Gamepad

Tiger King Coin Gamepad is a gaming ecosystem made for $TKING holders. A series of games allows for players to bet their $TKING and face off against others in exchange for winning the prize pot and NFT’s.

Buying on Uniswap

Step 1: Install MetaMask

Install MetaMask mobile app or the desktop extension for Chrome or FireFox browser and create your new wallet. If you already have another wallet, you can use the Import option by entering your seed phrase.

Step 2: Get ETH

If You don't have some ETH in your wallet you can buy ETH directly from MetaMask or transfer some from exchange (CoinBase, Binance, crypto.com, etc.).

Step 3: Uniswap

Go to Uniswap or use the Uniswap widget and Connect your wallet.

Step 4: Swap for TKING

1. Import contract address.
2. Swap ETH for TKING.
3. Set slippage to 0.5%.
4. Click Swap.

Buy on PancakeSwap

Step 1: Install MetaMask

Download MetaMask mobile app or install the Desktop extension for Chrome or FireFox browser, create or import wallet and activate the BSC network.

Step 2: Get BNB

If You don't have some BNB in your wallet you can buy and transfer BNB directly from exchanges/wallets like Binance, Crypto.com, TrustWallet or SafePal.

Step 3: PancakeSwap

Go to PancakeSwap and Connect your wallet.

Step 4: Swap Tokens

1. Import contract address.
2. Swap BNB for TKING.
3. Set slippage to 0.5%.
4. Click Swap.

Buying on XT.com

Step 1: Create an account

Go to XT.com or download their iOS/Android app and create an account by following their requirements. An account will automatically provide you a LV0 Unverified wallet.

Step 2: Get Verified

While logged in your XT.com account, under your profile navigation menu, select the Identification or Real-Name verification tab to be redirected to the Personal Verification page. Under LV1 Basic Verification, click the Verify button and follow their steps. If You wanna increase your account limits, you may opt for LV2 Advanced Verification after.

Step 3: Watch the video tutorial

Once your account gets verified, watch the step-by-step video tutorial provided by Nelson Does Crypto to deposit funds and swap to $TKING.
